A. Milne, M. McLaughlin, R. Tamstorf, A. Stomakhin, N. Burkard, M. Counsell, J. Canal, D. Komorowski, E. Goldberg.
Flesh, Flab, and Fascia Simulation on Zootopia. ACM SIGGRAPH 2016 (Talks).

The latest flesh simulation techniques developed for the anthropormorphic animals of Disney's Zootopia. The unique challenge was to couple the rich motion that can only be achieved by simulation with the stylized aesthetics of animation.
